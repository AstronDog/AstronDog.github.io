Education and Research

I grew up in Anhui province, eastern China. During 2010 and 2014, I studied Communication Engineering for my Bachelor degree at Hainan University, locating at the southest and the sceond largest island – Hainan Island. I then moved to northewesten China to study astonomy at Xinjiang Astronomical Observatory, Chinese Academy of Sciences, where I get my master degree in June 2017.

From November 2017, I began study pulsar astronomy at Bielefeld University, Germany and due to finish my PhD in Physics in 2020. My mainly research topic is on Pulsar Timing. I am now working within European Plusar Timing Array (EPTA) group.
